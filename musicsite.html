Meet Hoang-Ha

Hello, my name is Hoang-Ha LuuPham. I'm graduating June 2025 with a BA in Music. I've been playing alto saxophone for over 8 years and have performed in a wide range of groups such as SCU's jazz ensemble, wind ensemble, and with outside organizations like the San Jose Youth Symphony Jazz Ensemble and the San Jose Saxophone Choir.

Recently, I had the incredible opportunity to perform a solo on Miles Davis's "All Blues" for my professor, Bill Stevens, at his faculty recital. In addition to saxophone, I also play drums, guitar, and piano. I am excited to teach players of all levels!
